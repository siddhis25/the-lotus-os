# THE LOTUS OS - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## Project Name

THE LOTUS OS – AI Powered Skin, Hair & Weight Management Platform

## Version

1.0

## Product Vision

To build a premium AI-powered digital clinic platform that simplifies patient care, clinic operations, appointment management, and personalized wellness programs while creating a trusted online presence for Dr. Vanita's THE LOTUS.

## Mission

Deliver a modern healthcare experience by combining medical expertise, technology, automation, and artificial intelligence.

## Business Goals

### Short-Term (0–6 Months)

* Launch professional clinic website
* Online appointment booking
* Admin dashboard
* Digital patient records
* Improve local SEO

### Mid-Term (6–12 Months)

* Online consultation
* AI Skin Analyzer
* AI Hair Analyzer
* AI Weight Assessment
* Membership plans

### Long-Term (1–3 Years)

* Nationwide expansion
